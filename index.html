Pricing & Services

Your vehicle deserves the right level of care, inside and out. Below you’ll find a breakdown of my detailing services and optional add-ons, with pricing based on your vehicle size.

Interior Detail – Vacuum all surfaces, clean touchpoints, clean interior windows, and apply a protectant to keep your interior looking fresh.

Exterior Detail – Hand wash, clean windows inside and out, clean and protect wheels, and apply a ceramic spray that provides 6–8 weeks of paint protection.

Add-Ons

Enhance your detail with optional add-on services to give your vehicle an extra touch of care.

  • Seat Shampooing
  • Headlight Polish
  • Engine Bay Cleaning
  • Plastic Restoration / Shine

Whether it’s one vehicle or a whole family fleet, select your vehicle type and the services you’d like. Once you’ve made your selections, submit the form and I’ll review your request and get back to you to finalize your appointment.

Pricing for all services and add-ons depends on vehicle size — Sedan, Small SUV (2-row), or Large SUV / Van / Truck.
