Pricing & Services

Your vehicle deserves the right level of care, inside and out. Choose from my Silver and Gold detailing packages for the interior and exterior, with pricing based on your vehicle size.

Interior Packages

Silver Interior – Blow out, full vacuum, clean all touch points, window cleaning, plastic shine/protection, and carpet cleaning.

Gold Interior – Everything in Silver Interior, plus steam cleaning and cat/dog hair removal.

Exterior Packages

Silver Exterior – Hand wash, hand dry, window cleaning, tire shine, wheel detailing, and a ceramic spray for lasting paint protection.

Gold Exterior – Everything in Silver Exterior, plus plastic shine and headlight polish.

Add-Ons

Enhance your detail with optional add-on services to give your vehicle an extra touch of care.

  • Seat Shampooing
  • Headlight Restoration
  • Engine Bay Cleaning
  • Plastic Restoration / Shine

Whether it’s one vehicle or a whole family fleet, select your vehicle type and the packages you’d like. Once you’ve made your selections, submit the form and I’ll review your request and get back to you to finalize your appointment.

Pricing depends on vehicle size — Sedan, Small SUV (2-row), or Large SUV.
