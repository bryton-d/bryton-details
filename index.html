Pricing & Services

Your vehicle deserves the right level of care, inside and out. Choose an interior detail, an exterior detail, or a combined detail, with pricing based on your vehicle size. Want to go further? Add optional add-on services for an extra level of care.

Interior Detail

Blow out, full vacuum, clean all touch points, window cleaning, plastic shine/protection, and carpet cleaning.

Exterior Detail

Hand wash, hand dry, window cleaning, tire shine, wheel detailing, and a ceramic spray for lasting paint protection.

Combined Detail

The full interior and exterior detail together at a combined price.

Add-Ons

Enhance your detail with optional add-on services to give your vehicle an extra touch of care.

  • Seat Shampooing
  • Headlight Restoration
  • Engine Bay Cleaning
  • Plastic Restoration / Shine

Whether it’s one vehicle or a whole family fleet, select your vehicle type and the services you’d like. Once you’ve made your selections, submit the form and I’ll review your request and get back to you to finalize your appointment.

Pricing depends on vehicle size — Sedan, 2 Row SUV / Crossover, or 3 Row SUV / Van / Truck.
