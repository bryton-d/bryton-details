Pricing & Services

Whether you’re looking for a quick refresh or a full detail, I keep my pricing simple and transparent so you know exactly what to expect.

Exterior Detail – Hand wash, clean windows inside and out, and apply a ceramic spray that protects your paint for 2–3 months.

Interior Detail – Vacuum every surface, clean all touchpoints, refresh carpets, and finish with a protectant to keep things looking sharp.

  • Sedans – $100
  • Small SUVs – $150
  • Large SUVs, Vans, Trucks – $200

Add-Ons:

  • Seat Shampooing - $25 (sedans and small SUVs), $50 (Large SUVs and Vans)
  • Engine Bay Cleaning - $25
  • Headlight polish - $25
  • Plastic Trim Shine - $15
  • Wheel coating - $15

When booking an appointment, just mention any extras you’d like in the “Additional Info” section — and I’ll make sure your ride leaves looking like new.
