Pricing & Services

Your vehicle gets our complete full-detail treatment, inside and out, at one simple price based on its size.

What’s Included in Every Full Detail

Interior: blow out, full vacuum, clean all touch points, window cleaning, plastic shine/protection, and carpet cleaning.

Exterior: hand wash, hand dry, window cleaning, tire shine, wheel detailing, and a ceramic spray for lasting paint protection.

Full-Detail Pricing

  • Sedan — $150
  • 2 Row SUV / Crossover — $200
  • 3 Row SUV / Van / Truck — $250

Add-Ons

Enhance your detail with optional add-on services to give your vehicle an extra touch of care.

  • Seat Shampooing
  • Headlight Restoration
  • Engine Bay Cleaning
  • Plastic Restoration / Shine

Whether it’s one vehicle or a whole family fleet, select your vehicle type and add any extras you’d like. Once you’ve made your selections, submit the form and I’ll review your request and get back to you to finalize your appointment.

Pricing is a single full-detail rate per vehicle size — Sedan, 2 Row SUV / Crossover, or 3 Row SUV / Van / Truck.
