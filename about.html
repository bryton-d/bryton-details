About Bryton's Auto Detailing

Hi, I’m Bryton — a mobile auto detailer serving Delaware County. I’ve been around cars my whole life, and I love helping people keep theirs looking their best.

I come from a long line of car lovers, and whether it’s under the hood or on the hood, I’ve always enjoyed making a car shine. My own ride — a Ford Focus ST — gets treated like a show car, and I bring that same level of care to every vehicle I work on.

When I’m not detailing, I’m an insurance broker with State Farm, helping people protect what matters. Auto detailing may be my side hustle, but I take it seriously — it’s a passion and a craft I’m proud of.

When you book with me, you’re getting more than a clean car — you’re getting someone who takes pride in the work and treats your car like it’s his own.

– Bryton Henry
